# ILLO Assembly Guide

This guide walks you through assembling the ILLO UFO saucer. Assembly takes approximately 20-30 minutes.

## Before You Begin

### Prerequisites

- [ ] Completed wire harness ([Wire Harness Guide](ILLO_Wire_Harness_Guide.md))
- [ ] 3.7V LiPo battery (included in STEM Kit)
- [ ] Circuit Playground Bluefruit (included in STEM Kit, pre-programmed)
- [ ] Magnetic levitation platform (included in STEM Kit)

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Possible Cause | Solution |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| UFO won't power on | Battery not connected | Check JST cable connection |
| UFO won't power on | Battery depleted | Charge via USB-C port |
| UFO won't power on | Incorrect battery polarity | Remove and reinstall battery correctly |
| Screws won't drive in | Cross-threaded | Back out and restart carefully |
| Dome won't lock | Misaligned | Lift and reseat, then twist |
| Won't levitate | Too heavy | Check that no extra parts are inside |
| Won't levitate | Puck misaligned | Ensure magnetic puck is centered in base |
